Story with Coreference Chains

George , a fantastically wealthy businessman , passes away after an illness .

A week later , Jacques , George 's attorney , has gathered the two children and their respective attorneys together for an important meeting , which the family assumes will be the reading of the will .

Jacques reveals that for years George had a secret family in Jamaica . His frequent business trips were actually trips to see his other family .

Cary and Audrey , George 's children , are stunned to speechlessness .

Jacques carefully explains that George wanted his entire estate to go to the other family . Cary and Audrey will get nothing .
