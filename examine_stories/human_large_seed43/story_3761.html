Jeff and Jim , 2 peas in a pod . The best of friends that do everything together . After one of them won the lottery , and of course sharing half of it with their friend , they are just living life to the fullest . Sitting there one night needing something to do , bored out of their mind .

Then Jim spots it . Something they have never have done , because they could never afford it .

Mingled with rich people at a massive party , limited to only those with money . They decided this was it , the fun they had been looking for .

You have to look the part though . Have to fit in with everyone else . They needed snob appeal .

After getting their hair all done , they were ready . Ready to mix it up with high society , and be one with their riches .
