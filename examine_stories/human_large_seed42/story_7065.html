Matt is reading a letter while walking with Jude .

Matt starts laughing , because something in the letter makes him happy . His father has died , and he will receive a large inheritance as a result .

Jude is a bit disturbed by Matt 's lack of sadness upon learning of his father 's death . He thinks it 's odd that Matt only seems to be interested in the money he 'll now receive .

Matt tells Jude that his father never seemed interested in his life , and that is why he is not sad about his death . He also tells Jude that his father abused him when he was a child .

Matt asks Jude to have lunch with him to celebrate his windfall . Jude makes up an excuse so he can avoid having lunch with Matt . He can not shake the feeling that Matt is not a good person and can not be trusted .
