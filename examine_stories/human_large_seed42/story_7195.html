Story with Coreference Chains

Gerard picks Jennifer up in a bicycle rickshaw on the boardwalk in Atlantic City . He seems to be in a good mood and tries to strike up a conversation but she is in a rush , ignoring him , and looking through her purse for something .

The rickshaw pulls up outside of the Trump Taj Mahal casino with a crowd of people milling in front of it .

Jennifer looks down into her purse , apparently looking for money to pay for the ride , still ignoring Gerard . She pays , says thanks and gets off his bike .

Gerard is stunned by her behavior - and lack of a tip . But he is also intrigued by her .

Gerard leaves the casino entrance and heads back down the boardwalk for another fare .
