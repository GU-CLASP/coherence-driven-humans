Brian stood in the dark office shocked by the events unfolding around him . He knew they were in trouble , but the fear that gripped him was very intense and made him catch his breath .

Suddenly Joan 's phone rang . She answered it and it was the person they were hiding from on the other end . They were being hunted by this man and had no way to get away now .

She listened as the man described the room and told her who was in the room with her . His focus turned to Julia . He told her how Julia was his real target and all they had to do was make her leave and they would be free . Joan glanced at Julia but remained silent .

Julia saw Joan 's gaze and looked at her questioning what was being said . She did not realize she was already in the crosshairs of the mans rifle .

Matt was perched on a roof on the next building and had Julia in his rifle sight as he spoke calmly with Joan and laid out his demands . Either they could make Julia leave , or they would all be killed .

Tom tried to log into the companies computer system to get word out to the police since the phone lines had been cut , but he quickly realized the system had been taken over as well and he had no access .

Joan tried to negotiate with Matt and find other ways to end the standoff and asked what else he wanted .

Julia realized that it was her he was after and that if she did not give herself up , everyone would be killed and she resigned herself to leave the room .

Joan realized that by Matt 's description of the room he must be on the roof of the next building . She hung up the phone and turned to stare in his direction .
