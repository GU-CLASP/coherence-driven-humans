Story with Coreference Chains

After years of bloodshed and civil war , King Eric now holds the crown . A feast celebrating his coronation is held in the hall .

King Eric made many enemies getting the crown . Just one of many daily precautions he takes is having his food tested for poison .

He looks over his guests , wondering whom to trust and whom to fear .

King Eric is n't just being paranoid . His advisors Mark and David plot his murder , even while they feast on his food .

However , the actual author of the murder plot is David 's mother , who has been a bitter but secret enemy of King Eric 's family for decades . They ruined her father with a shady land deal and she had to grow up in poverty . No one suspects that the quiet , old woman has thought of nothing but revenge ever since .
