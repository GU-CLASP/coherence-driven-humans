Story with Coreference Chains

Father Joe appeared amidst the chaos with two off-duty soldiers . He proclaimed that a very special event was about to take place . He raised his hands and stood in front of a makeshift tabernacle . Explosions rocked the land behind him .

Just then a chopper descended from the sky . Instead of artillery and ammunition , it lowered a young calf .

The soldiers looked on in disbelief . A cow ? How is that going to help us in the fight ? The sun set and a sense of calm and confusion washed over the battlefield .

Martin clapped and smiled . He was the only one that knew what was going on . This was a ceremonial feast the villagers used to honor their ancestors . But now a feast to boost the morale of the soldiers .

Frederic rubbed his hands together expecting a good meal . Martin assured him that it would be the best he 's ever had .

Laurence and Albert were n't so sure . They were vegetarians and could n't eat anything being offered . Instead they grabbed a six pack of Budweiser and pulled out some playing cards . Something to pass the time .

Martin cracked open a Budweiser too . This was a celebration after all . Meanwhile the cow was made into a soup and ready .

The ceremonial soup was passed around along with plans for tomorrow 's battle . The men laughed as they sampled the soup while going over battle plans . They tried to guess the ingredients while strategizing for war .

As the men savored the soup , laughter rang out across the camp . The men temporarily forgot about the war . The strangeness and warmth that day brought to the battlefield would make it memorable , not because of its grandeur .
