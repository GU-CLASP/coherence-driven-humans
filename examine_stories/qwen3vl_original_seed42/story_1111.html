John stands before a mirror , adjusting his tie . In the reflection , Sarah lies asleep in bed , the room dimly lit by a bedside lamp .

The bedroom is quiet as Sarah stirs under rumpled sheets , her hand brushing the empty space beside her . Sunlight filters through the curtains , illuminating the disheveled bed .

Sarah rises and approaches John , her silhouette visible in the mirror as he smooths his shirt . He glances at her reflection , pausing briefly before resuming his routine .

Fully dressed in a crisp shirt and tie , John turns to face Sarah , who sits on the bed with her back to him . He buttons his cuffs , his posture tense yet deliberate .

Now in a tailored suit , John holds a briefcase and a hat , his gaze lingering on Sarah as she watches him from the bed . He hesitates at the door , then steps into the hallway , leaving the room behind .
