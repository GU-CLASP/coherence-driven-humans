The scene is set in hospital . A doctor talks to a nurse at a nurse 's station . A patient fills out a form . The nurse tells the doctor that two detectives came buy earlier to see him . They recommend he comes down to the station for questioning . The doctor is nervous . Has he been finally caught after all these years ?

Connie gently puts down a tape recorder at her desk . She knew she had him based on evidence , but wanted to get a taped confession . The doctor stands next to her without saying a word .

The doctor asks Connie , `` what is this all about ? '' Connie calmly says , `` Sit down . We have some questions for you . '' John feels sick in the doctor 's presence . There is an ominous red glow coming from a street sign outside . The station is otherwise dimly lit .

Connie takes off her coat and begins the interrogation . John pinches his nose . He wants to vomit . This is one of the sickest bastards he has ever encountered .

Connie says , `` We know what you did to those kids . We have you dead to rights . Confess and maybe the DA will take the death penalty off the table . '' The doctor smirks . Connie loses control of her emotions and pushes the doctor backwards and he tumbles from his chair . John sits motionless . He will say he saw nothing .
