After a lifetime of research , he had finally done it . Phillip 's life work was creating a device that could transport users into old photographs . He put on his best suit and transported himself into an old black and white photo . He was right there with his mother and her great-grandmother on her wedding day .

Phillip could n't believe how real each experience was . He was able to enter any photo he could find . He was transported to the location and was right there . This time he jumped into a scene with three of his uncles . All looking good in suits .

His next trip was into a photo from the 1900s . He was able to meet and speak with his great-grandmother . A woman he had never actually met in real life .

Next he went back even further . He got a chance to meet and talk with his great-grandmother 's extended family . This was like meeting your entire family tree in real time !

At the end of the day Phillip was exhausted , but felt exhilarated by this new technology . He took off his suit and began to catalog all of his old photos . He pinned them to the wall , making a map of all the new places he would explore . And smiled thinking about all of the new people within his family that he would finally meet for the first time .
