It had been a rough night for Emile . His drinking problem was becoming more serious , and , as he awoke from a long sleep , he realized with some embarrassment that he had slept on the floor the previous night .

Groggily , Emile reached his hand out in an attempt to get his bearings . His hand landed on a book that lay on a table .

Emile retrieved the book and flipped through it . It was the book his estranged wife had written . She had sent him an early copy , perhaps as a token of respect in spite of the difficulties their marriage now faced .

As he read the volume , Emile was hit with a flood of emotions . He still loved his wife .

Emile took a moment to engage in deep self-reflection . He knew he needed to get his life together .
