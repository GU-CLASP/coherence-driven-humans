Jack walked down a long , dimly lit corridor , his red jacket standing out against the drab background

As he turned a corner , he found himself in a vast , green room filled with rows of identical booths , the fluorescent lights above humming softly

The room seemed to stretch on forever , with no signs of life anywhere , and Jack 's footsteps echoed off the walls as he made his way deeper into the space

Suddenly , he saw a figure in the distance - it was Scatman , standing in front of a row of booths , his eyes fixed intently on something

Jack approached him cautiously , noticing that Scatman 's hands were shaking slightly as he held a small object

As Jack drew closer , Scatman looked up , his eyes locking onto Jack 's , and for a moment , they just stared at each other , the only sound the hum of the fluorescent lights .
