As Ellen picked up the paper , she noticed that it was a cryptic message that read : `` Meet me at the old warehouse at midnight . '' She wondered what it could mean and who Robert was .

Robert , unaware that Ellen had picked up the paper , continued on his way , glancing over his shoulder as if he was being followed . Ellen decided to follow him , curious about his mysterious behavior .

As they walked through the city streets , Ellen noticed that Robert was leading her to an abandoned alleyway . She quickened her pace , trying to keep up with him .

Suddenly , Robert ducked into a doorway , and Ellen followed him . Inside , she found herself face to face with Leonardo , a man with a menacing look in his eye .

It turned out that Robert and Leonardo were involved in some kind of shady deal , and Ellen had stumbled upon their plans . She knew she had to get out of there fast .

Ellen turned around to make a quick escape , but not before catching a glimpse of the two men 's suspicious conversation . She knew she had to warn someone about their plans .

With the paper still in hand , Ellen made her way back to her apartment , determined to uncover the truth about Robert and Leonardo 's mysterious activities .
