Story with Coreference Chains

Jack enters his hotel room after a long vacation day . He fears something does n't feel right about the room he had just entered and is frozen in fear .

Jack goes to the bathroom to splash cold water on his face , only to notice that there is someone in the bathtub .

As Jack stands at the door of the bathroom , a woman emerges from the bathtub and stares at Jack .

The woman walks up to Jack and goes for an embrace . Jack hugs her back , as she tells him that she asked the front desk for the key to his room to surprise him .

Just as Jack was about to ask her a question , the woman appear to become much older and started to chase him around the room saying that she loved him and that they were meant to be together .

Jack backs away from the bathroom telling the woman that he is a married man and that she needed to leave .
