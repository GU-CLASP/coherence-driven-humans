Story with Coreference Chains

Two soldiers are being blessed by a chaplain in the middle of a war zone . They are hoping that they are given strength for the battle ahead .

Above them , cattle is being flown in by a helicopter to give the soldiers supplies in the front lines .

As night falls , soldiers try to celebrate and get their minds off of the task that is ahead of them the next day .

A soldier sitting at one of the tables tries to tell a story to the others to help them ease up and laugh a little .

Frederic interrupts him however telling him that he should focus telling scary stories since they 're going to have to embrace the horrors that they 're about to see the next day . At least horror stories will set them in the right state of mind .

The other soldiers start to listen to Frederic tell his horror story .

But Martin interrupts him and tells everybody the twist of the horror story , spoiling the ending for everybody . Instead , Martin tells the others that they need to drink to get ready for tomorrow .

The others proceed to laugh and drink . Other soldiers take a look at some of the plans for tomorrow . The general takes a look at a piece of paper .

But when Frederic asks if the general is looking at some of the battle plans for tomorrow , the general replies `` yes '' . The general then adds , `` And here 's a twist for you , you 're going to be at the very front of the convoy of tanks . ''
