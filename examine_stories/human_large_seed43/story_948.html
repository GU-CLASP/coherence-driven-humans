A young woman , Natalie , and a young man , John , are standing in a large performance hall . The pair are ballet dancers and the hall is lined by mirrors with a grand piano in the corner . An older gentleman , Vincent , watches as they audition . He is the director of the ballet .

After a grueling dance session , Natalie looks to Vincent , anxiously awaiting any feedback .

Vincent looks down with a completely cold look on his face , completely ignoring Natalie .

Natalie 's face looks horrified . She imagines that her performance was terrible and Vincent is deeply disappointed in her .

She hurries back to the dressing room dejected , where many other dancers , including her rival Ksenia , are waiting for their chance to audition .
