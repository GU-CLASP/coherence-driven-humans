Reese had an immediate answer when Matthew , the teacher , asked the class to name some of the most important consequences of the invention of the Guttenberg press .

Reese always has an answer to whatever question Matthew asks , and he is reluctant to call on her again , but no one else in the class has anything to contribute .

Reese answers that if it were n't for the Guttenberg press , high school yearbooks might never have come into existence .

She explains how they are really the most important books being printed because they capture the most meaningful events that shape students ' lives .

She then mentions that she 's the editor of this year 's yearbook and she 's able to take pre-orders for anyone interested .
