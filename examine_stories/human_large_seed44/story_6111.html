The witching hour had hit at GroupLens . It was 3 PM and , like clockwork , Nick and Dexter had arrived at Jason 's place . What would the conversation for today be ? One thing you do know is that when 3 PM hits the lab the productivity hits the fan . Jason was nervous today though . He had been waiting to start his journey for the past 3 months and finally the day had come . It was the final day of classes before the December Holiday break . All his finals were done and assignments were in . Jason had only one more semester of college left . He knew that if he was ever going to graduate then he had to leave now in order to be back in time for the Spring 2026 semester . Little did he know what lay ahead of him in a trip that would change his life in ways he never imagined .

Nick had never climbed a mountain . He had conquered a few mountains , figuratively , in his life , but never had he seen the world from over 14,000 feet . While he had been on top numerous times in his life ( if you know what I mean ) , this would be the ultimate experience . As Nick prepared to leave , a tear came to his eye , knowing that he might never make it back alive to the city he loved .

More tears came to his eyes when he remembered he had invited his pesky friend Dexter to come along on his journey . Jason , being driven by the goal at hand , started envisioning their destination : the snowcapped peaks of Mt . Hood . It was no Everest , but when you have never been mountain climbing before , Oregon 's highest peak was exotic enough . Jason started to take mental inventory for the journey : parkas , sherpas , St. Bernards with multitudes of firewater ... these gentlemen were as ready as they ever could be .

But really , Jason 's biggest fear was n't the climb , the cold , the crevasses that had consumed so many climbers in recent years ... it was the journey-long separation from his first love , his true love : technology . With only a GPS and some crappy communication gadgets to appease his needs , this was going to be a very trying journey .

At this point in his musings , Jason became distracted by a leathery scraping sound . It slid through the car rhythmically , like the sound of a young maiden shucking corn . It was , however , the doom-laden soundtrack of red-eyed gremlins in a laborious crawl up Nick 's throat and into his lap . Doll-sized bundles of filth and fury , the bilious mal-creatures writhed into acclimation with the bright light of the car . Jason observed this unholy birth with shock-glazed calm .
