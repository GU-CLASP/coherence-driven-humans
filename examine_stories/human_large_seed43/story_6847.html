Ben slowly lowered his finger toward Louie . He tried to remain calm and pet Louie . But Louie was too excited . He leapt into Ben 's arms . Ben let out a terrified scream .

His mother Lin and sister Cameron watched . The two had seen this before . But it never gets easier . All they could do was watch Ben struggle to pet the small dog .

Ben and Louie wrestled on the ground . Louie meant no harm , but Ben was too afraid to realize it . He screamed and cried . Usually it 's the dog , but this time it was the man .
