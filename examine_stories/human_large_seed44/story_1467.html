Story with Coreference Chains

The continuous , circular wall of the inside of the command center is covered in 4 inch bubble lights , emitting a dull yellow glow . Rosario , the main researcher , stands on a landing platform , looking down at her worker Anthony . The lights reflect off her brown leather jacket and frame her in an eerie light . Rosario calls down to Anthony to begin the energy transfer .

Down below , Anthony looks at his transparent display providing information about the fusion reactor and its components . Seeing that all components meet safety checks , Anthony calls out that he 's moving to engage the fusion reactor .

Anthony looks down from his console and reaches down below to access the cabinet holding the fusion reactor 's ignition switch , a cylinder tube with grip handles .

Anthony grabs onto the reactor 's fusion switch and pushes it into the console , which engages the fusion reactor .

The dim bubble lights surrounding the inside of the command center all begin to slowly pulse , confirming the fusion reactor has begun powering up . The lights look like the eyes of a fly , witnessing this major breakthrough via a million reflections .
