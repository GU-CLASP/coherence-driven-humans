Story with Coreference Chains

John and Mary are sitting in a cheap local diner in the early 2000s . John is in his early 30s and is shaggily handsome with scruffy , soft medium straw blonde hair and is wearing a black leather jacket . They 're sitting opposite each other by the diner 's long windows , which have a yellow sign on them and outside of which there 's a view of a rundown tilted parking lot , trees and a white cement retail type building . John is leaning onto the table in between them looking intently at Mary , waiting for her to decide .

She is beautiful . Mary 's in her late 20s , thin and has lighter blonde hair in a messy bob , and is wearing a fitted white tank top with a butterfly outline on front . Her dark bra is visible through the tank top . An old two-story grey cement motel is behind her outside the diner windows . She has a black mug of coffee in front of her and is slouching a bit while looking sharply into John 's eyes , considering things .

John 's patience is wearing thin as he waits . Suddenly he leans back heavily onto his chair and his face looks confused and undecided as he reaches his hand into the inside of his jacket to pull out money to pay .

At the same time , Rocco comes into the diner and stops on the other side at the entrance beside the old green and black cash register . He 's about 35 , Italian , with thick dark black eyebrows and slicked back black hair . He 's tall and thin , wearing a black dress shirt with a dark dressy jacket over it . He has a serious , dour look on his face , and turns his head to stare at John and Mary . He 's been following them .

Mary does n't notice him and instead is still focusing on John . Clouds have covered the sun outside and now everything seems darker and greyer . She has decided .
