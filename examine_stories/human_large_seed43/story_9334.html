Harrison is carefully making his way down a muddy slope towards a river . He has to navigate the river bank and find a way through various trees and other vegetation .

Harrison finds a vine hanging from above and grabs it for an attempt to swing across the river instead of trying to wade or swim .

The vine did n't work to make it across the water and Harrison has to swim . He finds an object floating and grabs it .

Harrison lets go of the object and swims , against the current , towards his destination .

A group of natives carrying bows aims at Harrison as he continues to swim towards his plane . Some of the natives wade into the water while aiming their bows while the rest stay on the bank .

Harrison 's plane is floating on the water . Harrison reaches the plane and lays underneath it resting from his swim . Natives hidden in the trees along the river bank watch Harrison while aiming at least one weapon at him .
