Harrison sprinted through the dense jungle , leaping over the terrain while swatting branches and leaves out of the way . He was told there was nobody living on this part of the river but clearly that was not the case . He had to get out of there fast .

He did n't remember exactly where he left his plane on the river but he did n't have time to take out his map and plot a proper course . He could hear voices yelling behind him , getting louder . They were getting closer . Just when he started to think about having to turn and defend himself , he came up to the riverside and leaped in .

This was n't exactly safe . There were all sorts of ways to die in that river ... crocodiles , piranhas , snakes ... heck even drinking it might kill you . But staying out of the water was even more dangerous . He had to swim .

Once he got a little further out he paused to look around and get his bearings .

Looking back from where he came , he could see a group of indigenous people yelling and drawing their bows . They were not firing , however , so it must have been out of their range already . They probably did n't want to waste precious arrowheads on someone they could n't hit anyway . They 'd already gotten their point across .

Realizing he was safe from them , Harrison turned his attention back to finding his plane . It was n't long before he spotted it , a little downriver from where he expected but that was just fine with him . He needed to get out of there asap .
