The light haired man Jack looks down at his coffee , wondering what their next destination must be . He knows that Jane is not happy with their lodgings at the moment but they do not have many options . He has been avoiding telling her that they might have to continue driving until the next town . Their reception here has not been what he had hoped for . The two lane main street can be seen behind Jack through the diner window , the sun barely shining on a cloudy day .

Jane looks up at Jack and tries to figure out exactly what he is thinking . Her tank top has a thin red dragonfly shown on the front of her chest . This is a sign of their order , one that members use to communicate to each other . They have been hunted for so long that they have to be careful about how they communicate but symbols and art have been one method that they have used for years . Jane has not been happy with living on the road for so long and looks at Jack with disdain in her eyes . The motel that they stayed at last night can be seen through the windows behind her .

As Jack looks back at Jane , he spots danger walking through the diner door . He reaches into his leather jacket , ready to grab his weapon . He has been jumpy lately and does not want to overreact . If the other diners see that he has a weapon , it will not be good for their cover .

A dark haired man with a long nose walks up to the diner counter . The green and black register stands empty , the staff having walked into the back of the diner to get an order . The man wears a dark gray suit jacket with a black collared undershirt . He is trying to act natural but looks over at Jane and Jack 's table . They are the two that he has been hunting and tracking for the last two weeks . Finally , he has caught up . Now is not the time to fight though , there are too many people around .

Jane looks up at Jack and spots the dark haired man as he walks up to the counter from the corner of her eye . Something does not feel right to her and she knows that they are going to be forced to make a decision , run or fight . She is so tired of running and living this life . Why wont they leave her alone ?
