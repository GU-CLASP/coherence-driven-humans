Val , standing beside him , whispered something in his ear , and Robert nodded , his eyes scanning the room for something or someone . The air was thick with the hum of computers and the murmur of conversations .

As they made their way through the crowded room , the desks became more disorganized , papers scattered across the surfaces , and the workers more frantic . Robert and Val seemed to be searching for something , their eyes locked on some point in the distance .

The room grew darker , the shadows cast by the flickering fluorescent lights deepening , and the workers ' faces grew more intense , their brows furrowed in concentration . Robert and Val moved with purpose , their footsteps quiet on the carpeted floor .

Finally , they stopped in front of a desk , cluttered more than any other , and Robert reached out , his hand hovering over a stack of papers . Val looked up at him , a question in her eyes , and Robert nodded , his face set in determination .
