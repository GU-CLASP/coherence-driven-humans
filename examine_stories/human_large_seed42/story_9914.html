Story with Coreference Chains

Reese was in it to win it . Win what ? Did n't matter . As long as there were people around to beat , she was determined to come out on top . In this case , the setting was high school . History class . Senior year .

Matthew was a young teacher but had been around enough to know Reese 's type . Yeah , she knew her stuff but damn was she annoying . It did n't help that he found himself attracted to her . This was a problem , obviously , and he hated himself for even having those thoughts . He would have called on anyone else but nobody even bothered to raise their hands ; they knew Reese would be first .

Reese gave the answer . It was correct , of course . She was always correct . It gave her a bit of an attitude , knowing she could do no wrong .

Years later , as an accomplished attorney , she decided to run for office . Her opposition decided to do some research on her to see if there was any dirt they could dig up . Naturally , they looked through her yearbook .

They found nothing until the very last page . A photograph of the yearbook staff , forgotten for years but now brought into the light once more . Reese is front and center , of course . But surrounding her in the photo are four people with plaid shirts . They look normal enough , but it would later come out that they were running an extortion ring at the high school . Reese was the bait . They 'd frame teachers and keep quiet in exchange for better grades , good recommendations , and occasionally money . Reese had managed to keep her name clear , but this photo was enough for the opposing campaign to use against her . Guilt by association .
