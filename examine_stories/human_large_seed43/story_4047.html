Story with Coreference Chains

A young woman , Mary , is hanging up white sheets on clotheslines outdoors .

Nearby , her brother , John , is using an axe to chop some logs for firewood .

Their mother , Sue , comes outside of their old brick house and calls to Mary .

John watches them , and Sue tells Mary that she needs to fetch some water as fast as possible , because their younger sibling 's fever is spiking .

Mary runs off to the nearby well as John stares off into the distance towards the well .

Mary fills up a pot with water from the well .

John looks down to the ground , deep in contemplation and worry over whether or not their brother will make it .

Mary takes the filled up pot and runs back towards the house as quickly as she can .
