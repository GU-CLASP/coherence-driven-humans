He observes her sitting at a table . He feels a flicker of jealousy as she is sitting with another man . He is drawn to her and plans to meet her .

Kim and the other man appear to be having a disagreement about something at the table .

She gets up finally and leaves the restaurant , much to her companion 's protests .

James quickly follows her out , making sure to keep a safe distance from her . He does n't want her to notice him .

He hides behind a newspaper until she is a bit down the sidewalk . Then , he follows her . He slows down and offers her a lift . He does this in order to engage her in conversation and , hopefully , ask her out to dinner .
