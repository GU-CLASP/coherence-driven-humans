Story with Coreference Chains

Jack stares into the bathroom , a quizzical expression on his face .

The hotel bathroom , painted green , appears empty . A shower curtain obscures half of the bathtub .

Astonishingly , a naked woman suddenly appears to push back the shower curtain and steps out of the tub .

The woman embraces jack , who awkwardly returns the gesture , but then is horrified to discover that her form has transformed , becoming old and muddy .

The newly transformed old crone looms toward Jack .

A terrified and confused Jack spreads his arms wide , hoping to halt the woman 's advance .
