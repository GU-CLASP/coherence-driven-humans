Philip was walking through the hallway when he heard the sound of someone singing . He followed the noise and found himself in front of a door slightly ajar . Through the gap , he saw a man , Jude , inside a room , wearing headphones and singing with great enthusiasm . Philip couldn ’ t help but smile at the sight , finding the moment amusing .

Matt opened the door to the room where Jude was singing . He looked surprised to see Jude so engrossed in his music . Philip watched as Matt hesitated for a moment , seemingly unsure of what to do next .

Jude , oblivious to the interruption , continued singing . Philip , now fully visible to Matt , raised his hand in a gesture of greeting . Matt responded with a slight nod , still preoccupied with the unexpected scene .

Philip stepped closer to the door , peering in more intently . He was intrigued by Jude ’ s performance and the contrast between Jude ’ s enthusiasm and Matt ’ s seriousness . The room was filled with a warm , nostalgic ambiance , accentuated by the wooden decor and soft lighting .

Suddenly , Jude noticed Matt and Philip . He paused mid-song , removing his headphones and giving them a curious look . The room fell silent , and the three men exchanged glances , each processing the unexpected encounter .

Philip decided to step into the room , followed by Matt . They both watched Jude , who seemed to be deciding whether to resume singing or address his unexpected audience . The atmosphere was tense yet oddly harmonious , as if they were all part of an impromptu , unspoken performance .
