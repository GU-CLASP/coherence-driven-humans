The room seemed endless , with reflections of booths and lights stretching out in every direction , creating a dizzying effect .

Jack 's eyes scanned the room , searching for someone , and then he spotted a figure in the distance - it was Scatman , a man with a unique appearance , known for his singing style .

Scatman was walking towards Jack , his eyes locked on his , and as they met , Jack felt a sense of unease , as if he was being pulled into a world where nothing was as it seemed .

The two men stood facing each other , surrounded by the endless reflections of the mirrored room , and for a moment , time seemed to stand still .
