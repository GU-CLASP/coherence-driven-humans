Story with Coreference Chains

Emile lays with his head on the table , exhausted after researching all night for an important piece of information . The sun is shining through a window down on his sleeping head . He knows that he is close to making an important discovery that will tie his research together . Before falling asleep he was close to putting the pieces together but needed one more clue before falling asleep .

As Emile wakes up , a sentence that he 'd read last night flashes into his mind and suddenly all of the pieces fall into place . He reaches up towards the table for his book . The trees and grass can be seen through the door in the daytime .

Emile intently reads the book titled Tanaina Plantlore . His body has not been feeling right after his most recent foraging session in nature . His home is in disarray , mirroring the state within his own head . His hair is disheveled as he searches for the piece of information that is needed .

As Emile reads , he finds the piece of information that he needs , identifying a plant that he 'd recently consumed . The pain on his face matches the pain felt in his body . He has worked so hard to survive off of the land , his hands still dirty from the recent foraging trip and his body has been pushed to his breaking point .

As Emile realizes what the plant was that he recently consumed , the situation turns dire . The plants that his hunger forced him to eat were actually poisonous . The pain in his stomach , the weakness that he feels in his body , the mental fogginess are all signs of his impeding death . He raises his hand to cover his mouth to hold in the scream he wants to emit . The horror in his eyes point to this knowledge that his time on Earth is limited . He gave it his all to survive in the wilderness but one mistake is all that it took to undo all the work he put in to survive .
