Jennifer approaches her father , Gary , while he is in his study . He has been working for the last few hours and she 's waited all this time anxiously . She wants to ask him a question .

Gary hears her approaching and looks up from his work . He is mildly amused that she would come to him while he is in his study , which is unusual for her .

She gathers her strength and after getting his attention , asks him if she can become his understudy . He works in law and she would like to work in the same field . She knows the only way to break into the career is to learn from her father .

There is tension in the room as the question hangs in the air . Neither say a word for a bit . The silence is deafening .

Gary takes in her request . He 's very surprised she would ask such a question . He wants to appear like he 's not surprised so he focuses on his work with indifference .

He then decides to make sure she is serious about this request . He asks her a witty question with a semi smile on his face `` Are you sure you want to work for me ? You barely listen to what I tell you to do here . What makes you think you 'd do it at a workplace ? ''
