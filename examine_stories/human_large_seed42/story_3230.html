Tim thought for hours about what this weird phrase meant . He knew it had something to do with the bank robbery that he and his associates were planning . He could n't simply ask what it meant in the email . That would be evidence that could be used in court .

Tim began having second thoughts about the robbery . What if something went wrong ? He was n't cut out for prison .

He decided he would call off the robbery just as he heard footsteps coming up to his office . He knew that this could only be Sam coming up to talk about the plan .

Sam walked through the door with terrible news . Something had gone wrong already . Another member of the crew got cold feet about the robbery and killed most of the people involved . Only Sam and Tim were left . They needed to leave town before they were victims as well .
