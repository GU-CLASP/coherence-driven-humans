James peeks out go his window to see that the lights across the courtyard , in the apartment across from his has gone out . `` Where did he go ? '' James whispered to himself . What he had seen in that window moments before frightened him like he 's never been before .

With the telephone still in his hands , James had to think fast .

`` That 's it . I 'll go next door . '' James heads for the door .

As James heads for the door , he hears footsteps . The footsteps stop and the knob begins to turn .

Bound to his wheelchair , James quickly rolls himself into the bedroom and shuts the door .

No where to go , no one to help . James stares at his bedroom door . The footsteps are now heard from within his apartment . His heart pounds as the door slowly begins to open .
