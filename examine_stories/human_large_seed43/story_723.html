James had went away and was hold up in an unassuming brick building that someone turned into an apartment . He thought he could hide out there but he was being surveilled .

Despite hiding out , James was receiving these phone calls with threats about harming hum and getting their money . They always scared him very much .

James was so scared that he always kept the place very dark . He did not want anyone to know someone was inside .

James would sit and reflect all the time about how he ended up in this situation . He got too far in debt with the wrong people and now they are here to claim what is theirs . He is trying to find a way out .

Its very tough on him right now as he was injured on the job and is left to move around in a wheelchair . So if anyone comes after him , he will not be able to get away at all .

As he rolls around in this tiny apartment , it becomes more difficult for him to cope with his situation . He has seemingly lost it all , including his ability to move around .

All James has left is himself and it makes him sit around and think a lot . He has nothing better to do at this point in time .

James always makes sure that the door is securely locked so no one can get in and do more damage to him .
