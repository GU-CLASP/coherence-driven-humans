Ominous clouds rolled in as a news reporter began taping for a nightly broadcast . The cameraman frames the image with a church surrounded by barbed wire fencing . This was no ordinary church . It was the church on the grounds of a county prison . A place of newfound infamy .

This was the church where the infamous Charles Manson practiced while in prison . He was known for the grisly late 60s murders but later found religion while incarcerated . But the church was now known for something far more sinister .

Two detectives rummage through torn clothing . Some covered in blood . All found scattered around a church within a prison . They could not believe what they were seeing .

Inside the church they found an even more gruesome scene . Four policemen were hanging in the basement . All suffering the same wounds . All were found with one thing in their coat pockets : A single car key .

Just outside the prison , a man in a leather jacket driving a red convertible is about to exit the gates . He pulls out a keyring filled with keys . All are there except four . He sees another prison guard just as he 's about to leave . He grins , hiding his bloodstained key ring just out of sight . Somehow he got away with it . He smirked and sped off .
