The papers continued to flutter , casting eerie shadows on the walls as Robert moved deeper into the room . The sound of the papers rustling was like a gentle whisper , drawing him further in .

As he reached out to touch the papers , they paused in their dance , suspended in mid-air as if waiting for his next move . The sudden stillness was almost palpable .

Robert 's hand hovered , hesitant , and then the papers resumed their gentle flutter , filling the room once more with their soothing rustle . The movement seemed to slow , as if in response to his presence .

The papers finally came to rest , blanketing the floor in a crunchy layer , and Robert stood still , surrounded by the quiet aftermath of their dance . The room was silent , except for the faint rustle of a stray paper .
